AI-Powered Edge Computing Systems

We design and build rugged, AI-enabled edge computing platforms that allow businesses to process data locally, reduce latency, and scale operations faster across demanding environments.

What We Build

Our edge computing solutions combine high-performance processors, modern memory and storage technologies, and reliable connectivity — all packaged into ruggedized hardware designed for harsh, real-world deployments.

Edge AI Gateways

Compact and powerful gateways featuring Intel and NVIDIA processors, DDR5 memory, and NVMe SSD storage for real-time inference at the edge.

Industrial Sensors & Cameras

Integrated vision and sensor systems designed to capture and process data locally, enabling faster decisions without cloud dependency.

Rugged Mini-Servers

Fanless, sealed mini-servers built for harsh environments, offering high reliability, low power consumption, and enterprise-grade compute.

Connectivity & Integration

Built-in Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and cellular modules ensure secure, flexible connectivity while keeping processing close to the data source.

Why Edge AI?

Processing data locally near its source reduces latency, lowers bandwidth costs, improves privacy, and enables real-time decision-making. Our systems are engineered for continuous operation in industrial, retail, healthcare, and smart infrastructure environments.
